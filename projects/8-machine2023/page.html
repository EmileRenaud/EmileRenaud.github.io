Sherbrooke Machine for Engineering Games 2023

Overview Machine

Project Description

This year challenge was to build a machine that can move across the island with the help of bridge that the machine need to place itself. It also need to connect the islands with a cable. All the details of the competition are here.

Composed of 6 members in robotics and mechanical engineering, the Team Machine tried to make the best machine possible to complete this challenge. Even if our machine didn't perform as well as in the trainning sessions, we still manage to beat all the others participant by a lot.

Electrical Section

For second year in a row, I was in charge to design a custom PCB that please all the requirement of everyone. That mean : control a stupid amount of actuator on the smallest PCB possible... The PCB can handle up to 6 DC motors including two of them with quadrature encoder feedback. It can also power up to 12 servos with differents voltages (5V and 7.4V). Two of my custom DC-DC are used to power the servos. The motor's driver are setup with a custom circuit that allow us to reset the power of the drive.
