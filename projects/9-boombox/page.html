Boombox

CAD Boombox

Project Description

This is a project I made because none of my friend have a speaker loud enough to play good outside and that can entertain a small party. Based on old home theater speakers, I made a portable boombox that can be used with a battery. It can play for 8-20h depending of the volume.

The box can easily be laser cut and glued together. The back section is made of 3 sections; that's allow the two side sections to be correctly seal from behind
