Project Description

This is a project I made because none of my friends have a speaker loud enough to play well outside and that can entertain a small party. Based on old home theater speakers, I made a portable boombox that can be used on battery power. It can play for 8-20 hours depending of the volume.

The box can easily be laser cut and glued together. The back section is made of 3 sections; that allows the two side sections to be correctly seal from behind

Futur Upgrade

  • USB-C to recharge the battery
  • Replace the 25W amps by two 30W amplifiers
  • Add more batteries
